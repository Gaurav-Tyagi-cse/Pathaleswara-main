About Us

Pathaleswara Sadanam is located on Pathalaganga Road in Srisailam, India. It is a reasonable stopping place for visitors to the area. To accommodate visitors’ needs, this hotel provides air-conditioned rooms. The management authors of the plan understood the problems associated with visitors having long journeys and weary travel.
The facilities are provided for the guests to have a refreshing pause so that they can relax and continue with their journey later.
At Pathaleswara Sadanam, we emphasize hygiene, ease, and hospitality of the staff in the first place amongst others. Whether for business or leisure, we create an environment of peace, care and high quality service.
